MY SHORT WOT BIOGRAPHY
My real name is Damian and this is my World of Tanks biography. I'm playing WoT since 22.03.2012 and I love this game as much as I hate It. I heard about this game from television adviertising and decided to give it a try. WoT has changed a lot through these years just as me. I fall in love with quick combat and mostly intensive gameplay. Obviously my start with thos game wasn't the greatest and until today I'm raging because of SPG's and some special teammates but I never stop playing longer than a three-four months. The main reason I would someday throw this game away from my life is that WoT consumes a lot of time (seriously grinding every tank in the game with average skill takes like 10k hours). In the moment of writing (the end of 2017) I'm focusing on getting 3rd marks and play very effective on high tier tanks. For example I achive to be first in EU on Strv S1 by official Wargaming global rating.
